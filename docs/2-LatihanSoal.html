Bab 2, Latihan Soal

Dasar-Dasar Pendugaan Parameter

\(Y_1, Y_2, Y_3\) adalah contoh acak yang menyebar eksponensial dengan fungsi kepekatan peluang yang disebutkan di bawah:

\[ f(y)= \begin{cases} \frac{1}{\theta}e^{-y/\theta},& y>0\\ 0, & \mathrm{lainnya} \end{cases} \]

Kaji penduga \(\theta\) tersebut:

\[ \begin{aligned} \hat{\theta}_1&=Y_1 &\hat{\theta}_2&=\frac{Y_1+Y_2}{2}\\ \hat{\theta}_3&=\frac{Y_1+2Y_2}{3} &\hat{\theta}_4&=\mathrm{min}(Y_1,Y_2,Y_3)\\ \hat{\theta}_3&= Y_3 \end{aligned} \]

  1. Penduga mana yang tak bias?
  2. Dari semua penduga tak bias, mana yang memiliki ragam terkecil?

Karena \(Y_1, Y_2, Y_3\sim \mathrm{Eksponensial}\) maka:

\[ \begin{aligned} E (Y)&= \theta &V(Y)&=\theta^{2} \end{aligned} \]

Bagian A: bias

\[ \begin{aligned} E ({\widehat{\theta}}_{1})&=E (Y_1)=\theta& \text{(tidak bias)}\\ E ({\widehat{\theta}}_{2})&=E \left( \frac{Y_{1} + Y_{2}}{2} \right) = \frac{1}{2}\ E\left( Y_{1} + Y_{2} \right) = \theta & \text{(tidak bias)}\\ E ({\widehat{\theta}}_{3})&=E \left( \frac{Y_{1} + 2Y_{2}}{3} \right)= \frac{1}{3}\ E\left( Y_{1} + 2Y_{2} \right)=\theta &\text{(tidak bias)}\\ E ({\widehat{\theta}}_{5})&= E\left( \overline{Y} \right) = E\left( \frac{1}{3}\sum_{i = 1}^{3}Y_{i} \right) &\\ &= \frac{1}{3}.3.E\left( Y_{i} \right) = \theta &\text{(tidak bias)} \end{aligned} \]

Untuk \(\hat{\theta}_4\), penghitungan nilai harapan agak lebih kompleks. Perhatikan fungsi kepekatan peluang marginal statistik tataan minimum:

\[ g_{1}\left[ y_{(1)} \right]=nf\left[ y_{(1)} \right]\left( 1 - F\left[y_{(1)}\right]\right)^{n - 1},\ a < y_{(1)} < b \\ \]

Maka tentukan fungsi kumulatif dari \(y\) dan dari situ, ambil fungsi kepekatan peluang marginal tatistik tataan minimum:

\[ \begin{aligned} F\left[y_{(1)} \right] &= \int_{0}^{y_{(1)}}{f(y)\ dy} = \int_{0}^{y_{(1)}}{\frac{1}{\theta}e^{- \frac{y}{\theta}}\ dy} = \frac{1}{\theta}\int_{0}^{y_{(1)}}{e^{- \frac{y}{\theta}}\ dy}\\ &= \frac{1}{\theta}\left( - \theta e^{- \frac{y}{\theta}} \right){\huge|}^{y_{(1)}}_0= 1 - e^{- \frac{y_{(1)}}{\theta}},\ \ y_{(1)} > 0\\ g_{1}\left[ y_{(1)} \right]&=nf\left[ y_{(1)} \right]\left( 1 - F\left[y_{(1)}\right]\right)^{n - 1} = 3\left( \frac{1}{\theta}e^{- \frac{y_{(1)}}{\theta}} \right)\left\lbrack 1 - \left( 1 - e^{- \frac{y_{(1)}}{\theta}} \right) \right\rbrack^{3 - 1}\\ &= \frac{3}{\theta}e^{- \frac{y_{(1)}}{\theta}}.e^{- \frac{{2y}_{(1)}}{\theta}} = \frac{3}{\theta}e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}},\ \ y_{(1)} > 0 \end{aligned} \]

Lalu cari nilai harapannya:

\[ \begin{aligned} E\left[ y_{(1)} \right]&= \int_{0}^{\infty}{{y_{(1)}g}_{(1)}\left[ y_{(1)} \right]\ dy_{(1)}} = \int_{0}^{\infty}{y_{(1)}\ \frac{3}{\theta}e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}}dy_{(1)}}\\ &= \frac{3}{\theta}\int_{0}^{\infty}{y_{(1)}\ e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}}dy_{(1)}} \end{aligned} \]

Untuk menyelesaikan integral tersebut, dapat dilakukan integrasi parsial:

\[ \begin{aligned} u&= y_{(1)} & dv&= \exp\left[\frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}\right]\\ du&=dy_{(1)}& v&=\int \exp\left[-\frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}\right]dy_{(1)}\\ & & &=-\frac{\theta}{3}\exp\left[-\frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}\right] \end{aligned} \]

Maka:

\[ \begin{aligned} E\left[ y_{(1)} \right]&= \frac{3}{\theta}\left[uv {\huge|}^{\infty}_{0}- \int_{0}^{\infty}{v\ du} \right]\\ &=\frac{3}{\theta}\left[\left\{y_{(1)} - \frac{\theta}{3}e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}} \right\} {\huge|}^{\infty}_0 - \int_{0}^{\infty}\left\{- \frac{\theta}{3}e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}}\ dy_{(1)}\right\} \right]\\ &= \frac{3}{\theta}\left[\left\{0 - 0\right\} + \frac{\theta}{3}\int_{0}^{\infty}{e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}}\ dy_{(1)}} \right]\\ & = \frac{3}{\theta}\left[ \frac{\theta}{3}\left( - \frac{\theta}{3}e^{- \frac{3y_{(1)}}{\theta}} \right){\huge |}^{\infty}_{0}\right]\\ & = \frac{\theta}{3} \neq \theta\ \quad \text{(bias)} \end{aligned} \]

Bagian B: ragam

Karena \(\hat{\theta}_4\) bias, ragamnya tak perlu dicari.

\[ \begin{aligned} V\left({\widehat{\theta}}_{1}\right)&= V\left( Y_{1} \right)\\ &= \theta^{2}\\ V\left({\widehat{\theta}}_{2}\right)&=V\left( \frac{Y_{1} + Y_{2}}{2} \right) = \frac{1}{4}\ V\left( Y_{1} + Y_{2} \right) = \frac{1}{4}\cdot{2\theta}^{2}\\ &= \frac{1}{2}\theta^2\\ V\left({\widehat{\theta}}_{3}\right)&=V\left(\frac{Y_1+2Y_2}{3}\right)=\frac{1}{9}\left[V\left(Y_1\right) +4V\left(Y_2\right)\right]\\ &=\frac{5}{9}\theta^2\\ V\left({\widehat{\theta}}_{5}\right)&= V\left( \overline{Y} \right) = V\left( \frac{1}{3}\sum_{i = 1}^{3}Y_{i} \right)=\frac{1}{9}V\left(Y_1+Y_2+Y_3\right)\\ & =\frac{1}{9}\cdot 3V\left( Y_{i} \right) = \frac{1}{3}\theta^2 \end{aligned} \]

Maka, penduga tak bias dengan ragam terkecil adalah \(\hat{\theta}_5\).

Jika \(Y\sim\mathrm{Binomial}(n,p)\), maka \(\hat{p}_1=Y/n\) adalah penduga tak bias bagi \(p\). Penduga lain bagi \(p\) adalah \(\hat{p}_2=(Y+1)/(n+2)\).

  1. Tunjukkan bias dari \(\hat{p}_2\).
  2. Tunjukkan MSE (KTG) dari \(\hat{p}_1\) dan \(\hat{p}_2\).
  3. Di nilai p apa saja \(MSE(\hat{p}_1)<MSE(\hat{p}_2)\)?

Karena \(Y\ \sim\ \mathrm{Binomial}(n,\ p)\ \), maka:

\[ \begin{aligned} E(Y)&= np & V(Y)&= np(1 - p)\\ E\left( Y^{2} \right)&=V(Y)+\left[E(Y)\right]^2\\ &=np(1-p)+n^2p^2=np(1-p+np) \end{aligned} \]

Bias dari \(\hat{p}_2\)

Pertama, cari nilai harapan dari \(\hat{p}_2\):

\[ \begin{aligned} E\left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right)& = \ E\left( \frac{Y + 1}{n + 2} \right) = \frac{1}{n + 2}\ E(Y + 1) = \frac{np + 1}{n + 2} & \text{(bias)}\\ \end{aligned} \]

Lalu, bias tidak lain adalah perbedaan dari nilai harapan penduga dengan parameter yang hendak diduga. Maka hitung bias:

\[ \begin{aligned} B\left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right) &= E\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right) - p = \frac{np + 1}{n + 2} - p \\ &= \frac{np+1-np-2p}{n + 2}=\frac{1-2p}{n+2} \end{aligned} \]

MSE dari \(\hat{p}_1\) dan \(\hat{p}_2\)

Kuadrat tengah galat tidak lain adalah ragam ditambah bias kuadrat. Oleh karena itu, cari bias dan ragam \(\hat{p}_1\):

\[ \begin{aligned} E\left( {\widehat{p}}_{1} \right)&= E\left( \frac{Y}{n} \right) = \frac{1}{n}E(Y) = \frac{1}{n}(np) = p & \text{(tidak bias)}\\ E\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{1}^{2} \right)&= E\left( \frac{Y^{2}}{n^{2}} \right) = \frac{1}{n^{2}}E\left( Y^{2} \right) = \frac{1}{n^{2}}\left[ np(1 + np - p) \right] \\ &= \frac{p + np^{2} - p^{2}}{n} \end{aligned} \]

Oleh karena \(\hat{p}_1\) tak bias, MSE hanya memiliki komponen ragam:

\[ \begin{aligned} MSE\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{1} \right)&= \ V\left( {\widehat{p}}_{1} \right)=E\left( {{\widehat{p}}_{1}}^{2} \right) - \left\lbrack E\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{1} \right) \right\rbrack^{2} \\ &=\frac{p + np^{2} - p^{2}}{n} - p^{2} = \frac{p(1 - p)}{n} \end{aligned} \]

Bias dari \(\hat{p}_2\) sudah ditemukan, cari ragamnya:

\[ \begin{aligned} E\ \left({{\widehat{p}}_{2}}^{2} \right)&= E\left[ \frac{(Y + 1)^{2}}{{(n + 2)}^{2}} \right] = \frac{1}{{(n + 2)}^{2}}\ E\left( Y^{2} + 2Y + 1 \right)\\ &=\frac{1}{(n+2)^2}(np-np^2+n^2p^2+2np+1)\\ &= \frac{n^{2}p^{2} - np^{2} + 3np + 1}{{(n + 2)}^{2}}\\ V\left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right)&= E\ \left( {{\widehat{p}}_{2}}^{2} \right) - \left\lbrack E\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right) \right\rbrack^{2} \\ &= \frac{n^{2}p^{2} - np^{2} + 3np + 1}{(n + 2)^{2}} - \left( \frac{np + 1}{n + 2} \right)^{2}\\ &=\frac{n^{2}p^{2} - np^{2} + 3np + 1}{(n + 2)^{2}} - \frac{n^2p^2 + 2np+ 1}{(n + 2)^2}\\ &=\frac{np-np^2}{(n+2)^2}= \frac{np(1 - p)}{(n + 2)^{2}} \end{aligned} \]

Karena ragam dan bias sudah ditemukan, cari MSE:

\[ \begin{aligned} MSE\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right)&= \ V\left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right) + \left\lbrack B\left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right) \right\rbrack^{2} = \frac{np(1 - p)}{(n + 2)^{2}} + \left( \frac{1 - 2p}{n + 2} \right)^{2}\\ &=\frac{np(1 - p) + {(1 - 2p)}^{2}}{(n + 2)^{2}} \end{aligned} \]

Kapan \(MSE(\hat{p}_1)<MSE(\hat{p}_2)\)?

Kondisi tersebut sama dengan:

\[ \begin{aligned} MSE\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{2} \right)&< MSE\ \left( {\widehat{p}}_{1} \right)\\ \frac{np(1 - p) + (1 - 2p)^{2}}{(n + 2)^{2}}& < \frac{p(1 - p)}{n}\\ n^2p-n^2p^2+n-4np+4np^2&<(p-p^2)(n^2+4n+4)\\ n^2p-n^2p^2+n-4np+4np^2&<n^2p-n^2p^2+4np-4np^2+4p-4p^2\\ (4np^2+4np^2+4p^2)-(4np+4np+4p)+n&<(n^2p-n^2p^2)-(n^2-n^2p^2)\\ (8n+4)p^2-(8n+4)p+n&<0 \end{aligned} \]

Nilai p dapat diperoleh dari rumus abc:

\[p_{1,2} = \ \frac{(8n + 4) \pm \sqrt{{(8n + 4)}^{2} - 4(8n + 4)(n)}}{2(8n + 4)} = \frac{1}{2} \pm \sqrt{\frac{n + 1}{8n + 4}}\]

Jadi, nilai p mendekati 0.5.

Metode Momen

\(X\) adalah peubah acak dengan fungsi kepekatan peluang \(f(x\ | \ \theta)=\frac{2}{\theta^2}(\theta-x), \quad 0<x<\theta, \ \theta \in \Omega=(0,\theta)\)

Maka:

Tunjukkan \(f(x \ | \ \theta)\) adalah fkp.

Suatu fungsi tersebut fungsi kepekatan peluang atau fungsi massa peluang jika:

  • Fungsi diskret: \(\sum_{i = 1}^{n}{P\left( x_{i} \right) = 1}\)
  • Fungsi kontinu: \(\int_{- \infty}^{\infty}{f(x \ | \ \theta)}dx = 1\)

Maka, karena \(f(x \ | \ \theta)\) kontinu:

\[ \begin{aligned} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x \ | \ \theta)\ dx&= \int_{- \infty}^{0}{f(x\ | \ \theta)}\ dx + \int_{0}^{\theta}{f(x\ | \ \theta)}\ dx + \int_{\theta}^{\infty}{f(x\ | \ \theta)}\ dx\\ &=0 + \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\int_{0}^{\theta}(\theta -x)\ dx +0=\frac{2}{\theta^2}\left(\theta x -\frac{x^2}{2}\right){\huge |}_{0}^\theta\\ &=\frac{2}{\theta^2}\left(\theta^2-\frac{\theta^2}{2}-0\right)=2-1=1 \end{aligned} \]

Tunjukkan \(E_\theta(X)=\frac{\theta}{3}\) dan \(\mathrm{Var}_{\theta}(X)=\frac{\theta^2}{18}\).

Pertama, hitung nilai harapan:

\[ \begin{aligned} E_{\theta}(x)&= \int_{0}^{\theta}{x\ f(x\ | \ \theta)}dx = \int_{0}^{\theta}{x\ \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}(\theta - x)}dx = \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\int_{0}^{\theta}{\theta x - x^{2}}dx\\ &=\frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\left( \frac{\theta x^{2}}{2} - \frac{x^{3}}{3} \right){\huge|}^\theta_0= \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\left( \frac{\theta^{3}}{2} - \frac{\theta^{3}}{3} - 0 \right) = \theta - \frac{2\theta}{3}\\ &= \frac{\theta}{3}\quad \text{(terbukti)} \end{aligned} \]

Lalu, hitung ragam:

\[ \begin{aligned} E_{\theta}\left( x^{2} \right)&= \int_{0}^{\theta}{x^{2}\ f(x\ | \ \theta)}dx = \int_{0}^{\theta}{x^{2}\ \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}(\theta - x)}dx = \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\int_{0}^{\theta}{\theta x^{2} - x^{3}}dx\\ &= \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\left( \frac{\theta x^{3}}{3} - \frac{x^{4}}{4} \right){\huge |}^\theta_0 = \frac{2}{\theta^{2}}\left(\frac{\theta^{4}}{3} - \frac{\theta^{4}}{4} - 0 \right) = \frac{2\theta^{2}}{3} - \frac{\theta^{2}}{2}\\ &=\frac{\theta^{2}}{6}\\ V_{\theta}(x)&= E_{\theta}\left( x^{2} \right) - \left\lbrack E_{\theta}(x) \right\rbrack^{2} = \frac{\theta^{2}}{6} - \left\lbrack \frac{\theta}{3} \right\rbrack^{2} = \frac{\theta^{2}}{18}\quad \text{terbukti} \end{aligned} \]

Dari suatu contoh acak berukuran \(n\) dari \(f(x \ | \ \theta)\), cari penduga momen \(\theta\), \(\hat{\theta}\), dan tunjukkan penduga tersebut tak bias. Cari ragam penduga tersebut.

Tentukan 1 momen dari populasi \(\mu_{k}\):

\[ \mu_{1} = E\left( X^{1} \right) = \frac{\theta}{3} \]

Tentukan 1 momen dari contoh \(m_{k}\):

\[ m_{1} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}{X_{i}}^{1} = \bar{X} \]

Solusi dari sistem persamaan \(\mu_{k} = \ m_{k}\):

\[ \begin{aligned} \mu_{1} &= \ m_{1}\\ \frac{\theta}{3}\ &= \ \bar{X}\\ \theta & = 3\overline{X} \end{aligned} \]

Maka penduga bagi \(\theta\), adalah \({\hat{\theta}}_{m} = \ 3\overline{X}\).

\[ \begin{aligned} E\left( {\hat{\theta}}_{m} \right)&= E\left( 3\bar{X} \right) = 3E\left( \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}X_{i} \right) = \frac{3}{n}\cdot n E\left( X_{i} \right) &\\ &= 3\left( \frac{\theta}{3} \right) = \theta& \text{(tidak bias)}\\ V\ \left( {\hat{\theta}}_{m} \right)&= V\left( 3\bar{X} \right) = 9\ V\left( \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}X_{i} \right) = \frac{9}{n^{2}}\cdot nV\left( X_{i} \right) &\\ &= \frac{9}{n}\left( \frac{\theta^{2}}{18}\ \right) = \frac{\theta^{2}}{2n}& \end{aligned} \]
