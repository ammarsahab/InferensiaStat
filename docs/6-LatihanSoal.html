Bab 6

Statistik Cukup

Eksponen dengan Indikator

Misalkan \(X_{1},X_{2},\ldots,X_{n}\) adalah contoh acak dari sebaran dengan \(f_{X}\left( x \middle| \theta \right) = e^{- (x - \theta)}\mathbf{I}(x > \theta)\). Tunjukkan bahwa \(T = X_(1)\) adalah statistik cukup bagi \(\theta\).

Fkp bersama antara \(X_{1},X_{2},\ldots,X_{n}\) dengan syarat θ, \(f_{\underline{X}}(\underline{x}|\theta)\).

\[ \begin{aligned} f_{\underline{X}}\left( \underline{x} \middle| \theta \right) &= \prod_{i = 1}^{n}{f_{X}\left( x_{i} \middle| \theta \right)} = \prod_{i = 1}^{n}{e^{- (x - \theta)}\mathbf{I}(x > \theta)}\\ &= e^{- \left( x_{1} - \theta \right)} \times e^{- \left( x_{2} - \theta \right)} \times \ldots \times e^{- \left( x_{n} - \theta \right)}\\ &= e^{n\theta - \sum_{i = 1}^{n}x_{i}}\mathbf{I}\left( x_{i} > \theta \right),\ i = 1,2,\ldots,n \end{aligned} \]

Menggunakan teorema, fkp dari \(\ T\ = \ X_{(1)} = \min\left( X_{i} \right)\), \(f_{T}\left( t \middle| \theta \right)\).

Perhatikan kembali statistik tataan:

\[ \begin{aligned} f_{T}\left( t \middle| \theta \right)&= nf(x_{(1)})\left\lbrack 1 - F\left( x_{(1)} \right) \right\rbrack^{n - 1}\mathbf{I}\left( x_{(1)} > \theta \right)\\ &= ne^{- \left( x_{(1)} - \theta \right)}\left\lbrack 1 - \left( \int_{\theta}^{x_{(1)}}{e^{- (x - \theta)}dx} \right) \right\rbrack^{n - 1}\\ \end{aligned} \]

Gunakan substitusi:

\[ \begin{aligned} u&= \theta -x &\to d&u=-dx\\ x&=\theta &\to u&=\theta-\theta=0\\ x&=x_{(1)} &\to u&=\theta-x_{(1)}\\ \end{aligned} \]

Sehingga:

\[ F(y)=-\int_0^{\theta-x_{(1)}}e^u du=-e^{\theta-x_{(1)}}+e^0=1-e^{-\left[x_{(1)}-\theta\right]} \]

Maka masukkan integral yang telah diselesaikan:

\[ \begin{aligned} f_{T}\left( t \middle| \theta \right)&= ne^{- \left( x_{(1)} - \theta \right)}\left\lbrack 1 - \left( 1 - e^{- \left( x_{(1)} - \theta \right)} \right) \right\rbrack^{n - 1}\\ &= ne^{- \left( x_{(1)} - \theta \right)}\left\lbrack e^{- \left( x_{(1)} - \theta \right)} \right\rbrack^{n - 1}\\ &= ne^{- n\left( x_{(1)} - \theta \right)}\mathbf{I}\left( x_{(1)} > \theta \right)\\ &= ne^{- n\left( \min\left( x_{i} \right) - \theta \right)}\mathbf{I}\left( x_{i} > \theta \right),\ i = 1,2,\ldots,n \end{aligned} \]

Turunan \(f_{\underline{X}}(\underline{x}|T = t)\) sedemikian rupa sehingga tidak bergantung pada θ.

\[ \begin{aligned} f_{\underline{X}}\left( \underline{x} \middle| T = t \right) &= \frac{f_{\underline{X}}\left( \underline{x} \middle| \theta \right)}{f_{T}\left( t \middle| \theta \right)}\\ &= \frac{e^{n\theta - \sum_{i = 1}^{n}x_{i}}}{ne^{- n\left[ \min\left( x_{i} \right) - \theta \right]}}\\ &= \frac{e^{n[min\left( x_{i} \right)]}}{ne^{\sum_{i = 1}^{n}x_{i}}}\ \end{aligned} \]

Tidak bergantung pada \(\theta\).

Jadi, \(T\ = \ X_{(1)} = \min\left( X_{i} \right)\) merupakan statistik cukup bagi \(\theta\).

Pareto

Let \(Y_{1},Y_{2},\ldots,Y_{n}\) denote independent and identically distributed random variables from a Pareto distribution with parameters \(\alpha\) and \(\beta\). Then pdf, if \(\alpha, \beta > 0\),

\[ f_{Y}\left( y \middle| \alpha,\beta \right) = \begin{cases} \alpha\beta^{\alpha}y^{- (\alpha + 1)},& y \geq \beta \\ 0,& elsewhere \\ \end{cases} \]

If \(\beta\) is known, show that \(\prod_{i = 1}^{n}Y_{i}\) is sufficient for \(alpha\).

Fkp bersama antara \(Y_{1},Y_{2},\ldots,Y_{n}\) dengan syarat \(\alpha, \beta\), \(f_{\underline{Y}}(\underline{y}|\alpha,\ \beta\ )\).

\[ \begin{aligned} f_{\underline{Y}}(\underline{y}|\alpha,\ \beta\ )&= \prod_{i = 1}^{n}{f_{Y}\left( y_{i} \middle| \alpha,\beta \right)} = \prod_{i = 1}^{n}{\alpha\beta^{\alpha}{y_{i}}^{- (\alpha + 1)}}\\ &= \alpha\beta^{\alpha}{y_{1}}^{- (\alpha + 1)} \times \alpha\beta^{\alpha}{y_{2}}^{- (\alpha + 1)} \times \ldots \times \alpha\beta^{\alpha}{y_{n}}^{- (\alpha + 1)}\\ &= \alpha^{n}\beta^{n\alpha}\left( \prod_{i = 1}^{n}y_{i} \right)^{- (\alpha + 1)},\ y_{i} \geq \beta,\ i = 1,2,\ldots,n \end{aligned} \]

Menggunakan Fisher-Neyman Factorization Theorem, definisikan fungsi-fungsi g dan h sedemikian rupa.

\[ \begin{aligned} f_{\underline{Y}}(\underline{y}|\alpha,\ \beta\ )&= \alpha^{n}\beta^{n\alpha}\left( \prod_{i = 1}^{n}y_{i} \right)^{- (\alpha + 1)} \times 1\\ &== g\lbrack T(y_{1},y_{2},\ldots,y_{n})|\alpha\rbrack \times h(y_{1},y_{2},\ldots,y_{n})$ \end{aligned} \]

Maka \(g\lbrack T(y_{1},y_{2},\ldots,y_{n})|\alpha\rbrack = \alpha^{n}\beta^{n\alpha}\left( \prod_{i = 1}^{n}y_{i} \right)^{- (\alpha + 1)}\) dan \(h(y_{1},y_{2},\ldots,y_{n}) = 1\)

Jadi, \(T(y_{1},y_{2},\ldots,y_{n}) = \ \prod_{i = 1}^{n}Y_{i}\) merupakan statistik cukup bagi \(\alpha\).

Penduga Konsisten

Misalkan \(Y_{1},Y_{2},\cdots,Y_{n}\) merupakan peubah acak dari sebaran Normal dengan nilai tengah \(\mu\) dan ragam \(\sigma^{2} < \infty\). Tunjukan bahwa \({\overline{Y}}_{n} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}y_{i}\) adalah penduga konsisten bagi \(\mu\).

Notasi \({\overline{Y}}_{n}\) menunjukkan bahwa \(\overline{Y}\) dihitung menggunakan sampel sebanyak \(n\).

\[ \begin{aligned} E\left( {\overline{Y}}_{n} \right)&= \mu\\ Var\left( {\overline{Y}}_{n} \right) &= \frac{\sigma^{2}}{n}\\ \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}{Var\left( {\widehat{\theta}}_{n} \right)}& = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}{Var\left( {\overline{Y}}_{n} \right)} = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty}\frac{\sigma^{2}}{n} = 0 \end{aligned} \]

Jadi, \({\overline{Y}}_{n} = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}y_{i}\) adalah penduga konsisten bagi \(\mu\).
